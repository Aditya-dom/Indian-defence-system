Indian defence System

The Indian Armed Forces are the militray forces of the Republic of India. It consists of three professional uniformed services: the Indian Army,Indian Navy and Indian Air force. Additionally, the Indian Armed Forces are supported by the Indian coast Guard and paramilitary . organisations Assam Rifles and Special frontier force and various interservices commands and institutions such as the startegic forces command , the Andaman and Nicobar Command and the Integrated Defence Staff. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Armed forces. The Indian Armed Forces are under the management of the Ministry of Defence(MOD). of the Government of India. with strength of over 1.4 million active personal , it is the world's second largest military. and has the world's fourth largest defence budget.
